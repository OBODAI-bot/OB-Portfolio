About Me

I'm a passionate Ghanaian Frontend Developer with a 1 year of experience building scalable solutions that address real challenges across Ghana. My journey in technology began at Ipmc University College, where I discovered my calling in creating innovative digital solutions for emerging markets.

Based in Kasoa, I've had the privilege of working with leading fintech startups and healthtech companies across Ghana. I specialize in building robust applications that handle millions of transactions, from mobile money integrations to healthcare management systems that serve thousands of patients daily.

What drives me is the opportunity to leverage technology for social impact. I believe in writing clean, maintainable code that not only meets technical requirements but also creates meaningful change in people's lives. My expertise spans JavaScript ecosystems, particularly React and Node.js, with a strong focus on building solutions optimized for African infrastructure and user needs.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me mentoring young developers through tech communities like Developers in Vogue and GhanaTech Lab, contributing to open-source African tech projects, or exploring local tech hubs across the continent. I'm passionate about building the next generation of Ghana tech talent and creating solutions that truly understand our unique challenges and opportunities.
